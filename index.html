Pro Tips

Master the art of AI generation with these expert strategies.

01. Be Specific

Replace generic words with precise descriptions. Instead of "a car", say "a matte black Porsche 911 GT3 RS".

02. Add Lighting

Always describe the lighting. "Golden hour backlighting with lens flare" creates more impactful results than no lighting info.

03. Describe the Camera

Include camera details like "85mm portrait lens, shallow depth of field, f/1.4 bokeh" for photorealistic results.

04. Set the Mood

Use emotional descriptors: "melancholic", "energetic", "mysterious". This guides the AI's color and tone choices.

05. Use Style References

Reference known styles: "in the style of Blade Runner", "Studio Ghibli aesthetic", "Wes Anderson color palette".

06. Layer Your Details

Build your prompt in layers: subject first, then environment, then lighting, then mood. This creates coherent results.
